Sidhu Moose Wala

Biography

He was a well-known Indian singer, songwriter, and actor associated with Punjabi music and cinema. His real name was Shubhdeep Singh Sidhu. He gained significant popularity for his distinctive style in Punjabi music, often incorporating elements of hip-hop. Sidhu Moose Wala's songs frequently touched on themes of Punjabi culture, social issues, and his personal experiences.
